About Nathan Long

Nathan Long is a Digital Design artist who creates bold, colourful artwork based around popular culture such as anime, gaming, music, films and even traditional style tattoo art. His work is stylised and often based around different themes such as his “Yu-Gi-Oh” and “Dragon Ball” series in which he draws artwork all inspired by the same universe. He takes inspiration from Francis Bacon, Andy Warhol whilst also taking inspiration from modern tattoo artists such as @daniel_kickflip_tattoer and @msgink.
